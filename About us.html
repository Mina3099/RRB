ABOUT US


Railway Recruitment Board, Bilaspur was established in September 2008 as the twentieth Railway Recruitment Board of the country. This organization works under the Railway Recruitment Control Board, New Delhi. At present a total of two gazetted officers and a total of twelve non-gazetted employees are posted in Railway Recruitment Board, Bilaspur. The Chief of the Railway Recruitment Board, Bilaspur is the Chairman and under his guidance the functions of the Railway Recruitment Board, Bilaspur are being performed by a team of Assistant Secretary and Category-C employees.

The office of Railway Recruitment Board, Bilaspur started in a small building located at RTS Colony of South East Central Railway, Bilaspur. Subsequently this office was shifted to the building located in the headquarters complex of South East Central Railway, where it was established as an independent office. Due to large scale conduct of recruitment examinations and lack of space in this office, a lot of problems were faced. To solve these problems, a proposal was initiated for construction of a new building for the Railway Recruitment Board, Bilaspur. After completion of the building construction, it was inaugurated on 26.02.2016 and handed over to Railway Recruitment Board, Bilaspur.

On 27.07.2016, Railway Recruitment Board shifted to the newly constructed building and since then this office is functioning smoothly in the newly constructed building. The new building of Railway Recruitment Board, Bilaspur is a two-storey building with modern facilities. This building has well equipped rooms for all officers, and well organized office rooms, meeting room, a huge examination room for document verification and psychological testing. This building is one of the beautiful buildings of South East Central Railway and is a very secure complex.

Railway Recruitment Board, Bilaspur issue notification for Category-C posts for Bilaspur, Raipur and Nagpur Divisions, Headquarters, Wagon Repair Shop Raipur, Motibagh Workshop Nagpur, General Store Depot Raipur under South East Central Railway Bilaspur and Nagpur Division of Central Railway. Thus, most part of Chhattisgarh state, some parts of Madhya Pradesh, Orissa and Maharashtra falls under the jurisdiction of Railway Recruitment Board, Bilaspur.

Railway Recruitment Board, Bilaspur conducts recruitment of Technical/ Non-Technical and Paramedical categories of Category-C as per the requisition submitted by Principal Chief Personnel Officer/South East Central Railway and Principal Chief Personnel Officer/Central Railway. The office of Railway Recruitment Board, Bilaspur is open from 09:30 AM to 18:00 PM from Monday to Friday. Closed on Saturday and Sunday, weekly holidays and gazetted/general holidays.
